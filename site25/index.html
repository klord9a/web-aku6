About

Adam Wainey

Adam Wainey is a real estate broker located in Palm Beach County and Miami-Dade County. This real estate broker was established back in 2006 with the goal of building a team of like-minded professionals that are committed to delivering the best customer service to all clients.

After 25 years of industry experience, Adam Wainey has learned that it is the relationships he creates with his clients that matter most. He believes that the people you work with make all the difference and he wants to be one of those people for you!
