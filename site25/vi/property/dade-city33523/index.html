Mô tả

THE POSSIBILITIES ARE ENDLESS!!! Fully remodeled multi family!!!!!! Own this beautiful 4 unit apartment complex with 8 bedrooms on Lake Iola. These apartments would make great vacation rentals or short term rental units. Property has been well maintained for 20+ years with the current owners. All apartments are super clean with newer carpets, laminate flooring and appliances. Front units have a screen enclosure and screen/vinyl window enclosed porch on the rear units. The owners former unit features a large open kitchen with plenty of cabinets, large rectangle living room and plenty of closet space all done in earth tones. There is a huge detached two car/two story garage with a Rec room having a partial kitchen & full bathroom. This space can be turned into a managers office, banquet room, game room etc. The owner has also added an extra large partially covered concrete entertaining area with railing overlooking the lake, fire pit and barbecue. Property also has a Laundry room and work/storage shed in the back. Lake Iola is a private lake and is one of three ski-able lakes in Pasco county. Imagine coming home and being able to walk out your front door ready to ski, fish, kayak, paddle board, swim or just relax in your lounge chair soaking up the sun. Then in the evening enjoy a cocktail and watch the colorful sky as the sunsets on the lake. All this, while your tenants pay your mortgage for you.
