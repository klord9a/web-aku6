About me

I am a licensed Realtor in Miami, Florida. I have been in the industry for over thirty years and have dealt with all aspects of real estate. I specialize in residential properties and can provide you with best guidance and advice possible.

I was born in the Dominican Republic and moved to Miami when I was eight years old. My parents were very ambitious people who left their family behind to pursue a better life here in America. They instilled a work ethic in me that has made me successful today and is what I pass on to my children.

After graduating from high school with honors, I decided it was time for me to make my dreams a reality. With the help of hard work and perseverance, I was able to transfer schools and finish my degree while also balancing being a full-time nanny. This taught me that there are no limitations when it comes to following your dreams.

I am now determined more than ever before because I know that success is right around the corner just waiting for me to take it!
